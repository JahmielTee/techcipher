How to care for your child's first teeth

Use a soft cloth to wipe gums from birth. Start brushing with a tiny smear of fluoride toothpaste as soon as the first tooth appears. Regular checkups from age 1 help prevent problems.

Brushing tips for kids

  • Brush twice daily for 2 minutes.
  • Use a pea-sized amount of toothpaste for children over 3.
  • Supervise until children can brush effectively on their own.

When to see an orthodontist

Early evaluation around age 7 is recommended to detect growth issues and intervene early when needed.
